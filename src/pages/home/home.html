Here are some things that help you to maintain good mental health, and which can help us when we start to feel down:

 Get involved and try out new things Keep active & eat healthily Appreciate what you have Accept yourself for who you are Develop your creative side Stay in touch with friends and family Talk to someone you trust Find some time each day to relax Set little goals and work towards them Drink alcohol in moderation Ask for and accept help
